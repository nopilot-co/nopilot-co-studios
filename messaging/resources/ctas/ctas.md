# CTA library

Approved call-to-action phrasings. A message carries **exactly one** CTA
(`one_cta` in the ruleset). The `compose` skill picks one matched to the
purpose and the relationship temperature, then adapts it to the brand's voice.

Prefer low-friction, specific asks over "let me know your thoughts."

## Low-friction (cold / early)

| Slug | Phrasing |
|------|----------|
| `worth-a-look` | "Worth a quick look — want me to send the 2-minute version?" |
| `yes-no` | "Is reducing {pain} on your roadmap this quarter?" |
| `open-door` | "Happy to share what we've seen work — open to it?" |

## Medium (engaged)

| Slug | Phrasing |
|------|----------|
| `15-min` | "Open to 15 minutes next week? Tuesday or Thursday?" |
| `send-detail` | "Want the case study? I'll send it over." |

## High-intent (warm / late)

| Slug | Phrasing |
|------|----------|
| `book` | "Grab a time that works: {link}" |
| `start` | "Want me to set up a trial workspace for your team?" |

## Break-up

| Slug | Phrasing |
|------|----------|
| `close-loop` | "I'll assume the timing's off and close the loop — reach out anytime." |

> Add phrasings by appending rows. Keep each to one sentence and one ask.
