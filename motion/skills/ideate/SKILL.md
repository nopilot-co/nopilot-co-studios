# ideate

**Purpose.** Turn the story spine into 2–3 concrete, distinct concepts and choose
one. Each concept names: the **archetype** (`explainer` / `infographic` / `flow`
/ `timeline` / `presenter`), the visual approach, the moments that animate, and
the rough shape (duration, aspect, with/without a presenter).

**Output (judgment).** The chosen concept + rationale, enough to storyboard from.

**Drives.** Nothing deterministic.

> Status: planned (contract only in S0). See `motion/CLAUDE.md` build sequence.
